## About

Hi. I write programs that are *unreasonably small* and look *unreasonably good*.

My thing is **Demoscene** — the underground art movement where you pack procedural graphics, a full audio synthesizer, and animation into a single 4KB or 64KB executable. No assets. No engines. Just math.

Taking CS at UMGC while working part-time. Nobody in my classes knows what a demoparty is. That's fine. I know.

---

## What I actually do

- Write `GLSL` fragment shaders that generate entire worlds from sine waves and SDFs
- Hand-roll `x86 ASM` stubs to shave 40 bytes from an exe
- Write custom FM synths that live entirely in a 200-line `.c` file — no stdlib
- Obsess over the gap between what *is* possible with 4096 bytes and what people *think* is possible

**Side quest:** Building a tracker-style sequencer on a Raspberry Pi Zero with a 128×64 OLED. Also slowly learning Rust. Slowly.
